STRETFORD

Stretford, located just a few miles south-west of Manchester city centre, is a town rich in history and community spirit. Once famed for its market gardens and vast contribution to Manchester's industrial growth, Stretford today is perhaps best known for its close proximity to Old Trafford, the iconic home of Manchester United.

The town is in the midst of an exciting transformation, with new developments revitalising Stretford Mall, its town centre, and the picturesque Bridgewater Canal. With a blend of suburban charm, historic landmarks like Stretford Public Hall, and a forward-thinking vision, Stretford is rapidly emerging as a thriving hub of local life, culture, and commerce. It also has it's own Adidas trainer!

Highlights

Old Trafford Football Ground
Home to Manchester United and Stretford's most famous export. If you're lucky, grab a ticket for a game. If you're not, visit the museum and tour.

The Trafford Centre
Shopaholic? Go to the Trafford Centre. Filled with world-famous designer brands and high street stores - there's plenty to choose from.

Imperial War Museum North
Learn the story of how war has affected British lives since 1914. The architecture and exhibitions in the state-of-the-art museum are world-class.

Legoland Discovery Centre
Immerse yourself in a series of interactive features where imaginations can run wild with creative inspiration at every turn.

Emirates Old Trafford
The home to Lancashire County Cricket Club and host to regular England international cricket matches. A state-of-the-art stadium well worth a visit.
