STRETFORD

Stretford, located just a few miles south-west of Manchester city centre, is a town rich in history and community spirit. Once famed for its market gardens and vast contribution to Manchester's industrial growth, Stretford today is perhaps best known for its close proximity to Old Trafford, the iconic home of Manchester United.

The town is in the midst of an exciting transformation, with new developments revitalising Stretford Mall, its town centre, and the picturesque Bridgewater Canal. With a blend of suburban charm, historic landmarks like Stretford Public Hall, and a forward-thinking vision, Stretford is rapidly emerging as a thriving hub of local life, culture, and commerce. It also has it's own Adidas trainer!
